About Me

Who are you and what's your background?

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Gabriel Germano and I am an 18 year old fullstack web developer with a passion for creating intuitive and engaging web applications. Although I do not have previous industry experience, I am constantly learning and building new projects to improve my skills.

I specialize in developing applications using modern web development technologies such as Angular and Spring Boot. I have experience working with databases such as MongoDB and MySQL and I am confident in my ability to create efficient and scalable backend systems.

Currently, I am seeking an internship opportunity to further develop my skills and gain real-world experience in the field of web development. I am eager to collaborate with other developers and designers to create innovative solutions that meet the needs of clients and users.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio. Please feel free to explore my projects and reach out to me with any questions or opportunities to collaborate.

What makes you different from other developers out there?

I am eager to learn and always looking forward to continuously expand my knowledge of the latest technologies, best practices, and industry trends. I am also a great problem solver who can communicate my ideas well, and looks to find creative solutions to complex problems in innovative ways.

What technologies do you have knowledge of?

Backend: Java, Spring Boot, MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB.

Frontend: Angular, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap.

Tools: Git, Github, Docker, Junit, RabbitMQ.

Additional: Restful API's, AWS, Bash, Linux.

What are some dream projects you'd love to work on?

  • A Chat App that can that allows users to communicate in real-time, and would have features such as group chats, videos and voice call, and file sharing.

  • A Bug Tracker that allows developers to easily track and manage bugs and issues that arise during the development process. This would be specially interesting since i can use it as a tool while building other projects.

  • Develop a RESTful API for a real-world problem or application. This could involve creating endpoints for user authentication, data retrieval, and database management.

  • Building a Satellite App that allow users to get the position of satellites in real time. The app could include features such as satellite identification, orbit visualization, and predictions of upcoming satellite passes.

    I've always been into space and astronomy stuff, so i'm currently figuring out ways i can include it in my upcoming projects.
